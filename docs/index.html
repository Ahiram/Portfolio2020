Introduction

I am a postdoctral neuroscience researcher at the University of Tokyo. My research question is how neural circuits generate and regulate rhythms and patterns. To address this issue, I focus on Drosophila larval motor circuits using optogenetics, connectomics, behavior assay and so on.

Researches

Neural circuits which generate rhythmical pattern in typical locomotion.

Typical animal locomotion is achieved by rhythmical pattern of motor neurons which is triggerde by command neurons and generated by central pattern generator. To reveal a comprephensive circuit from a brain to muscles, I choose Drosophila larvae as model animal because they have only 10000 neurons and optogenetics and connectomics techniques are available. I work to understand brain-muscles neural circuit which innervates animal locomotion from the point of views of function, behavior, genetics, and anatomy.
